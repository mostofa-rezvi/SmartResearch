# Product Roadmap: ResearchBridge (SRCP)

## Step 1: The Foundation (Chapter 1)
- [ ] Welcome Page: Clean, purpose-driven landing page.
- [ ] Registration Flow: Streamlined, capturing essential academic details.
- [ ] Authentication Core: Secure login with deliberate "friction" requiring login after email verification.

## Step 2: The Gate (Chapter 2)
- [ ] Login with Password: Secure initial authentication.
- [ ] Two-Step OTP Verification: Send one-time passcode to email and verify before granting access.

## Step 3: The Four Kinds of People (Chapter 3)
- [ ] RBAC Foundation: Define Super Admin, Admin, Users, and Invited Users.
- [ ] Invitation System: Super Admin exclusive capability to invite verified academics.

## Step 4: The First Conversation (Chapter 4)
- [ ] Mandatory Onboarding Form: Extract research interests and keywords.
- [ ] Recommendation Lock: Block feed/community suggestions until `onboarding_completed=true`.

## Step 5: The Living Room (Chapter 5)
- [ ] Q&A Foundation: Implement structured questions, answers, and the upvote ranking system.
- [ ] Open Thoughts: Enable micro-blogging and reflection posts for researchers.
- [ ] Groups: Build public/private group spaces with isolated discussion feeds.

## Step 6: The Library (Chapter 6)
- [ ] Journal Directory: Build extensive database of Q1, Q2, and Q3 journals.
- [ ] Deep Taxonomy: Implement category/subcategory system for life sciences, social sciences, etc.
- [ ] Geo & Institutional Filtering: Filter journals by publisher region and entity type.

## Step 7: The Discovery Engine (Chapter 7)
- [ ] Personalized Search: Integrate Elasticsearch with onboarding profile for boosted relevance.
- [ ] Research Metadata: Surface abstracts, DOI, and journal quality tiers in results.
- [ ] Library Integration: Implement saving papers and following authors.

## Step 8: The Face (Chapter 8)
- [ ] Academic CV: Build the standard researcher profile with activity timelines and keywords.
- [ ] Professional Profile: Build the verified professional profile for Invited Users with affiliations and editorial roles.

## Step 9: Collaboration & Knowledge-Sharing (Upcoming)
- [ ] Shared collaborative spaces.
- [ ] Paper discovery & validation for amateur and seasoned scientists.
